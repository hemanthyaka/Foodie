Do you Know about BURGER ?

A hamburger or simply burger is a food consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. Hamburgers are often served with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon, or chilis; condiments such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, relish, or a "special sauce", often a variation of Thousand Island dressing; and are frequently placed on sesame seed buns. A hamburger patty topped with cheese is called a cheeseburger. Hamburgers are often sold at fast-food restaurants and diners, but are also sold at various other restaurants. There are many international and regional variations of hamburger. Some of the largest multinational fast-food chains have a burger as one of their core products: McDonald's Big Mac and Burger King's Whopper have become global icons of American culture.

Location: MG Road, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India


Cuisine:

Spice Haven celebrates the vibrant flavors of South Indian cuisine with a modern twist. Our menu showcases a fusion of traditional Andhra, Telangana, and Tamil Nadu culinary traditions, featuring an array of aromatic spices and locally sourced ingredients. From spicy curries to flavorful biryanis and dosas, every dish at Spice Haven is a culinary journey through the rich tapestry of South Indian flavors.


Ambiance:

Nestled in the heart of Vijayawada, Spice Haven offers a welcoming and elegant ambiance, blending contemporary design elements with subtle nods to South Indian culture. Warm earthy tones, intricate wood carvings, and subtle lighting create an inviting space where guests can relax and indulge in a memorable dining experience.

Signature Dishes:


Hyderabadi Dum Biryani:

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with succulent pieces of marinated chicken or mutton, layered with aromatic spices and slow-cooked to perfection in the traditional dum style. Andhra Chilli Chicken: Tender chicken pieces marinated in a spicy blend of Andhra spices, stir-fried with onions, peppers, and curry leaves for a burst of flavor.


Pesarattu Dosa:

A healthy and delicious dosa made from green gram lentils, topped with finely chopped onions, green chilies, and served with coconut chutney and spicy tomato chutney. Gongura Mutton Curry: A tangy and flavorful curry made with tender mutton pieces cooked in a spicy gravy infused with the unique tartness of gongura (sorrel) leaves.


Live Dosa Station:

Experience the art of dosa-making with our live dosa station, where skilled chefs prepare a variety of dosas to order, customized to your preference. Weekend Buffet: Indulge in our weekend buffet featuring an extensive selection of South Indian delicacies, including vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, desserts, and refreshing beverages. Catering Services: Spice Haven offers catering services for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions, bringing the flavors of South Indian cuisine to your doorstep with personalized menus and impeccable service.


Reservations:

Reservations are recommended, especially during peak hours and weekends. Guests can make reservations by calling our restaurant or through our website.
